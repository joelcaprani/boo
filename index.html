The Proposal

“You’re thirty and not engaged” is the last thing Joel said to me before he proposed. We had to walk Riggs the day of the proposal, before our dinner reservation for my thirty-first birthday and we were running very late. After the walk Joel wanted to sit for a bit and "cool down", I refused! We didn’t have time. He insisted so Joel and Riggs sat down on the table top of a picnic bench overlooking our dog park in Rosedale. Riggs was looking at me with his tongue out - exhausted from fetching. Joel was looking at me and I was looking up at them annoyed – hangry. Joel reached into his pocket and pulled out a small box and Riggs’ ears perked up, probably thought it was treat for him but it was a treat for me! He opened my present and looked at me and said “You’re thirty and not engaged, will you marry me?” I laughed out loud then called him a jerk and said ‘Yes!!’. My special birthday proposal, the most beautiful engagement ring and the best carbonara dish.
